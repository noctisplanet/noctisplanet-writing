Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Writing ("the App") is an English-grammar practice app for Chinese speakers. This policy explains what happens to your data. The short version: the App has no server of ours, collects nothing, and keeps everything on your device.

No developer server, no data collection

The developer of Writing does not operate any backend server for the App. We do not collect, receive, store, transmit, or have access to your personal data, your practice content, your scores, or your API keys. There is no account, no login, and no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDK in the App.

Where your data lives

  • Papers, questions, answers, scores, and progress are stored locally on your device.
  • Your LLM API key(s) are encrypted and stored on your device.
  • None of the above is ever sent to the developer.

Third-party LLM providers (you bring your own key)

The App does not include any AI model or API key. To generate papers, grade answers, or analyze sentences, you configure your own API key for a third-party Large Language Model provider (for example, DeepSeek or Alibaba Qwen).

When you use those features, the App sends the relevant content - the Chinese prompt sentence and your English answer - directly from your device to the provider you selected, authenticated with your own API key. The request does not pass through any server operated by us.

That content is then processed by the third-party provider under their own privacy policy and terms. We have no control over, and take no responsibility for, how those providers handle your data. Please review the policy of the provider you use, for example:

  • DeepSeek: https://www.deepseek.com
  • Alibaba Qwen (Model Studio): https://www.aliyun.com

You are billed directly by your chosen provider for API usage. The App does not process any payment and takes no commission.

Text-to-speech

The read-aloud feature uses the operating system's built-in speech synthesis on your device. No text is sent to us.

Children's privacy

The App collects no data and is not directed at children specifically. Using the AI features requires a third-party API key and account, which have their own age and eligibility requirements.

Deleting your data

Because all data stays on your device, you can remove everything by deleting the App, or by clearing individual papers and your stored key from within the App.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
