FAQ

Papers won't generate / grading does nothing.
Writing has no built-in AI model. You must add your own LLM API key in Settings first. Open Settings -> a provider (DeepSeek or Alibaba Qwen) -> sign in with your API key. Without a valid key the AI features cannot run.

Which providers are supported?
DeepSeek and Alibaba Qwen (OpenAI-compatible chat-completion APIs).

Where is my key stored? Is my data uploaded?
Your key is encrypted and kept on your device, as are your papers. The app has no server of ours; your content goes directly to the LLM provider you chose, using your own key. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How am I billed?
The app is a one-time purchase. API usage is billed by your chosen provider directly - the app takes no commission.
